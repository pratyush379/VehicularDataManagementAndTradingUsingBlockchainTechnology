Vehicular Data Management and Trading

Using Blockchain Technology


About the Project


In our this work we have tried to establish trust between car owners and the potential buyer of second hand car. Because of increasing cases of frauds in trading of second hand vehicle such as tempering odometer , hiding miantenence record , hiding faults in car and presenting fake registration or insurance document ,it becomes hard to believe any third party in market. So We have tried to establish trust by secureing records of car such as maintainence , repair,accident etc. in form of digital registry, where the record remains forever and resistant to manipulation. Overall, our implementation builds on top of the Blockchain and Ethereum protocols. we have used solidity to write and implement smart contracts. Once any data in added to our system it can not be manipulated or updated later on.
